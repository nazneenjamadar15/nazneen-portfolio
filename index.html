What I Do

I work as a Customer Success Engineer in a startup, managing the complete customer lifecycle—from product demos and PoCs to onboarding, support, and long-term retention.

I start by delivering product demos and supporting pre-sales validation, followed by leading Proof of Concepts (PoCs) where I handle setup, testing, and guide customers through evaluation to production.

During onboarding, I work closely with customers to configure DNS, CDN, and WAF, ensuring a smooth go-live and faster time-to-value.

Post go-live, I handle support tickets and L2 troubleshooting, ensuring timely resolution and a seamless customer experience. I also stay engaged through QBRs, sharing insights and helping customers optimize their setup.

I actively work on customer retention, satisfaction, and account growth by identifying upsell and cross-sell opportunities.

To improve adoption at scale, I create knowledge base articles, product videos, and conduct training sessions for customers and partners, helping reduce repetitive queries.
